How the scenario is calculated

Monthly context hours = people × weekly hours × 4.33. Recovered work applies only the recovery share you deliberately choose. The self-reported readiness index uses unbenchmarked heuristic weights: 40% centralization, 40% governance, and 20% tool-surface pressure. Payback appears only after recovery is above zero and divides the setup scenario by modeled monthly value. Validate every input against observed baselines before using any number in a buying decision.
