# Market, Content, And Sales Execution Prompt v2

Use this to update ArchFlow market, ICP, content, and sales assets.

## Core Positioning

ArchFlow turns messy product-team material into evidence-backed PRDs, task packets, and a maintained knowledge base. Tools make PMs draft faster. ArchFlow removes the PRD production burden and makes context compound.

## Target ICP

- B2B SaaS scaleups and product-led companies.
- Roughly 50-500 employees.
- Teams with 2-5 PMs.
- Buyers: Director or VP Product, Head of Product, Product Ops, and senior PM leaders.

## Content Rule

Use the latest owner-approved mix:

- 70% static educational/analytical posts.
- 20% carousel/checklist/template posts.
- 10% short video or scripted demo posts.

Every video must have a static companion.

## Required Outputs

- ICP card.
- Competitor objection scripts.
- Static template library.
- Five-week content calendar.
- Discovery call script.
- Qualification checklist.
- Sales motion from diagnostic to PRD Rescue Sprint to retainer.
- Demand metrics separating attention from payment or firm intent.
