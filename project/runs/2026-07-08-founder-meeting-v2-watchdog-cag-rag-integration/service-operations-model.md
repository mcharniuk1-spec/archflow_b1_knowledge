# Service Operations Model

Status: integrated planning artifact.

## Operating Model

ArchFlow is framed first as a customer-discovery-to-PRD operating service:

1. Intake and source boundary.
2. JTBD, 90-day story, and 45-minute discovery.
3. Evidence digest using FACT / INTERPRETATION / HYPOTHESIS / GAP.
4. Measurable PRD with Definition of Done.
5. Markdown/JSON task packet.
6. AF Review gate.
7. Client handoff.
8. KB promotion only after review.
9. Weekly learning loop from delivery, content, and discovery.

## Offer Ladder

### Discovery Diagnostic

Output: pain map, workaround map, readiness score, evidence gaps, next package recommendation.

Status: research/free or low-friction test first. Paid diagnostic is a hypothesis until E7 evidence exists.

### PRD Rescue Sprint

Output: source inventory, context digest, evidence-backed PRD, acceptance criteria, Definition of Done, task package, review report, and KB update candidate.

Status: first paid package candidate.

### Monthly PRD/KB Operating Retainer

Output: recurring PRD updates, backlog handoffs, decision/risk log, monthly KB update, and optional approved content/sales snippets.

Status: later offer after clean sprint samples and buyer-risk evidence.

## QA Gates

Every client-facing artifact must include:

- Source boundary.
- Facts separated from interpretations and hypotheses.
- Source refs for material claims.
- No raw private material.
- Measurable acceptance criteria.
- Definition of Done.
- Existing alternatives/status quo.
- Buyer trigger, anxiety, workaround, and switching context.
- Task packet.
- AF Review verdict.
- Demand/ROI/customer claims downgraded unless E6/E7 evidence exists.

## Evidence Handling

- Internal E1 proof is delivery capability evidence, not demand evidence.
- E2 evidence cards are outreach qualification evidence, not buyer validation.
- E4 engagement is attention unless paired with buyer-risk signals.
- E6/E7 demand evidence requires source-packet willingness, paid diagnostic interest, proposal request, budget-owner referral, prepayment, approved paid start, or firm paid intent.
- Raw cards and drafts remain run evidence; reviewed conclusions only move to WikiLLM through promotion rules.
