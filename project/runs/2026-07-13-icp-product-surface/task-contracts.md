## External-action gates

- Deployment and Figma synchronization require an explicit deployment instruction.
- One provider call requires durable nonce/spend enforcement plus separate owner approval.
- Outreach, publication, buyer contact, and payment actions require reviewed targets and owner approval.
- Vault or source-system writeback requires current connector/tool proof, least privilege, and a readback receipt.
