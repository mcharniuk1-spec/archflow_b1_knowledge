## Source Discipline

- Grade: A for product-category facts because each cited page is a current first-party public source.
- Limitation: each statement is vendor positioning, not independent evidence of product quality, buyer demand, comparative performance, customer outcomes, pricing, security suitability, or fit for a particular account.
- Cycle’s evidence is documentation-level rather than a full current marketing-page capture; its capability statement is deliberately narrow.
- The status quo, junior PM/BA, and fractional product consultant are human or process alternatives, so they have no vendor page in this register. They are modeled as comparison categories rather than factual product claims.
