# E5.3 Review Gate

Date: 2026-07-06
Status: ready for owner or AF Review

## Review Question

Should the E5.3 inbound qualification model be accepted as the no-code scoring
gate for early ArchFlow inbound signals?

## Accept If

- The 100-point model reflects the first ICP and the Product
  Discovery-to-Production PRD Pack offer.
- The bands make it clear that attention is not demand.
- The fields are safe enough for public run artifacts when real identities and
  raw lead text are omitted.
- The next-action values match the current owner-review and outreach gates.

## Revise If

- The score gives too much weight to generic engagement.
- The first ICP definition changes before E6.
- The diagnostic form asks materially different questions from the current
  E5.2 event schema.
- A CRM, analytics, or Notion workflow is approved and needs different fields.

## Blocked Until Approval

- Analytics installation.
- CRM or spreadsheet storage of real leads.
- Notion writeback.
- Diagnostic form implementation.
- Outreach or follow-up messages.
- Any claim that a signal proves demand.

## Next Safe Action

Use this model to draft the E3 diagnostic form field map or E6 outreach review
criteria, without collecting real leads or publishing anything.
