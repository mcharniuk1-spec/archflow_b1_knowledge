# E1.5 Public Reporting Gate

Date: 2026-06-30
Status: started, gate defined, publishing blocked until approval

## Purpose

E1.5 starts by defining the public-safe process for case-study and content reporting. This is not public posting yet. It is the gate that decides what can become a carousel, report, or post.

## Audience Default

FACT: The current public-facing audience is product leaders in B2B SaaS scaleups: Directors or VPs of Product, Heads of Product, Product Ops leads, and senior PM leaders in companies with roughly 50-500 employees and 2-5 PMs.

INTERPRETATION: Public reporting should educate that audience about source-grounded PRD workflow quality, not present a founder diary or generic AI automation content.

## Post Identity Default

HYPOTHESIS: Posts should default to ArchFlow company content, not personal build-in-public content, until the owner explicitly chooses a personal voice lane.

## GloomyLord Boundary

GloomyLord is an internal visual/reporting sidecar by default. It can design report templates, carousel structures, dashboard-friendly visual systems, and proof cards. It is not public-facing branding unless separately approved.

Visual style default: product-system with research-lab discipline. Use clean process diagrams, artifact before/after blocks, evidence labels, redaction bars, status chips, and high-contrast mobile-readable layouts. Avoid fake dashboards, private screenshots, target-company logos, hype visuals, or theatrical styling.

## Carousel Eligibility

A task can become a public carousel only when all of these pass:

1. Approved public-safe source artifacts exist.
2. The output has a clear evidence label: FACT, INTERPRETATION, HYPOTHESIS, or GAP.
3. It does not claim demand, customer proof, revenue, ROI, or market validation unless E6/E7 evidence exists.
4. It contains no raw private material, private links, credentials, operational IDs, local paths, or named target accounts.
5. AF Copy drafts from approved evidence only.
6. AF Review approves safety, claim status, English-only text, and audience fit.
7. Owner approves final carousel text and post copy before publishing.

## Channel Default

LinkedIn first. X and Threads should use adapted excerpts after LinkedIn copy passes review.

## CTA Default

Education-first until the diagnostic page and E3 rules are approved. The safest CTA is a low-friction question about PRD/source-material bottlenecks or an invitation to discuss workflow fit after the diagnostic path exists.

## Blocking Questions

1. Should the first hosted dashboard be public, hidden-link, or auth-gated?
2. Should first public posts link to the dashboard, a future diagnostic page, or no live asset yet?
3. Is GloomyLord ever public-facing, or always internal role language?
4. Is the approver model owner-only, AF Review plus owner, or another named review path?
5. Are pre-E3 CTAs allowed, or should posts stay education-only until diagnostic page readiness?
