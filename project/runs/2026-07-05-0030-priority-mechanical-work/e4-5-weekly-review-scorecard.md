content review after E4 publication begins. It can also be used before
publication to check whether drafts route toward the correct evidence.

## Review Questions

FACT:
- Which posts or drafts exist, and where are they recorded?
- Which checks or approvals passed?

INTERPRETATION:
- Which theme most clearly explained the PRD Pack value?
- Which CTA best routed people toward a concrete workflow question?

HYPOTHESIS:
- Which buyer pain seems strongest based on responses?
- Which content angle should be tested next?

GAP:
- What is still unsupported by evidence?
- What requires owner approval, AF Review, E2 evidence, or E6 outreach before
  being used again?

## Decision Rule

| Condition | Decision |
|---|---|
| Attention only, no buyer-risk signal | Keep learning, do not claim demand. |
| Source-packet willingness or diagnostic interest appears | Create an E6 follow-up packet for owner approval. |
| Privacy/source-boundary objections appear | Feed objections into E2/E3 positioning and diagnostic copy. |
| Unsupported claim performs well | Do not reuse until evidence catches up. |
| Low engagement but high-quality buyer question appears | Treat as stronger than broad attention. |
