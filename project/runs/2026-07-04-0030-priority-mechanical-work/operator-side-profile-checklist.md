## Public-Safe Completion Evidence

After approved execution, record only:

- Which channel was updated.
- Which approved draft source was used.
- Whether a public-safe link was included.
- Whether a visual was used from an approved public-safe artifact.
- Checks performed after update.

Do not record:

- Private account IDs.
- Private URLs.
- Credentials or tokens.
- Screenshots that include private UI state.
- Platform analytics before E5/E7 gates.

## Stop Conditions

Stop and record GAP if:

- The owner has not selected the voice lane, link target, visual policy, and timing.
- A platform asks for sensitive profile/account values.
- The copy requires a demand, ROI, customer, or revenue claim.
- The update requires external publication beyond the approved profile fields.
