## Interpretation

ArchFlow should position against current alternatives by ownership boundary rather than feature count:

- Product management suites own roadmaps, feedback, and planning systems.
- Insight repositories own searchable research and customer-signal stores.
- AI PRD tools own faster drafting and coaching.
- Workspace AI owns enterprise search, agents, and context access.
- Human alternatives own manual synthesis and expert judgment.

ArchFlow's current wedge is the service-plus-method path: approved source packet to evidence-backed PRD, task packet, KB handoff, and review gate.

## Content Pillars To Test

1. PRD bottleneck is source lineage, not writing speed.
2. Roadmap suites do not automatically turn messy discovery into delivery-ready specs.
3. Insight repositories still need translation into acceptance criteria and backlog.
4. AI PRD tools draft faster, but review gates and KB handoff are the missing production layer.
5. Workspace AI can find context, but product teams still need an accountable artifact owner.
