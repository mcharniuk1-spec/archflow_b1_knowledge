# Competitor Objection Script Template

Status: reusable positioning and sales template.

Use this to compare ArchFlow against current alternatives without unsupported competitor claims.

## Competitor / Alternative

- Name or category:

- Claim status: FACT / INTERPRETATION / HYPOTHESIS / GAP

## Categories To Cover

- Productboard / Aha / Cycle: roadmap and product-management suites.
- Dovetail / BuildBetter: insight repositories and VoC workflows.
- ChatPRD: faster PRD drafting.
- Notion AI / Glean / Dust: workspace AI/search.
- Status quo: meetings, docs, scattered notes, repeated clarification.
- Junior PM or BA: internal production support with supervision needs.
- Fractional product consultant: leadership/strategy support rather than recurring PRD/KB production.

## Script

When buyers say they already have an alternative:

- Acknowledge the alternative.
- State ArchFlow's job: source packet -> evidence-backed PRD -> task packet -> KB handoff.
- Name the remaining risk that the alternative may not own.
- Offer a low-friction source-packet review.

## Review Gate

- No unsupported competitor claims.
- No ROI guarantee.
- No customer/demand proof claim unless E7 evidence exists.
