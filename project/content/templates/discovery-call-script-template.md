# Discovery Call Script Template

Status: reusable E5/E7 sales-discovery template.

Default length: 45 minutes.

## 0-5 Minutes - Context

- What changed recently that made product knowledge harder or riskier to recover?
- Is the forcing moment an enterprise questionnaire/RFP, onboarding or hiring wave, key-person departure, or something else?
- Who owns the answer across Product, Security, Sales Engineering, RevOps, and leadership?
- What source material would you be willing to share for a reviewed diagnostic?

## 5-15 Minutes - 90-Day Story

- Tell me about a specific questionnaire, onboarding, departure, feature, or handoff from the last 90 days.
- What source material existed?
- Where did context get lost?
- What rework or delay happened?

## 15-25 Minutes - Current Alternatives

- How do you solve this today?
- Which tools or people are involved?
- What works?
- What still requires senior product attention?

## 25-35 Minutes - Customer Forces

- Push: what is making the current process painful?
- Pull: what would a better process make possible?
- Anxiety: what would make you hesitate to outsource or systematize this?
- Habit: what makes the current way hard to leave?

## 35-42 Minutes - Value And Budget Path

- What would a good output need to include?
- What Definition of Done would make the knowledge spine and generated output trustworthy?
- Who would approve a paid Knowledge Diagnostic or Product Knowledge Reliability Sprint?
- What budget path exists?

## 42-45 Minutes - Next Step

- Confirm source-packet boundary.
- Offer a Forcing-Moment Knowledge Diagnostic or Product Knowledge Reliability Sprint only if fit is real.
- Record gaps and owner decisions.
