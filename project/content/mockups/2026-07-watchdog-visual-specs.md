# 2026-07 Watchdog Visual Specifications

Status: public-safe design specifications; no rendered social assets
Date: 2026-07-10

## Design Direction

Use the current ArchFlow website/dashboard direction: an ivory editorial field, teal architectural system visual, blue process language, dark operational panels, and compact evidence-first hierarchy. The content should feel like a product-system brief for B2B SaaS product teams, not generic AI-agency promotion.

| Token | Value | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Ivory field | `#f4efe3` or `#fbf7ed` | Editorial background and low-noise space |
| Teal architecture | `#0f716e` | Arch/system motif and process signal |
| Process blue | `#244e6f` | Stage rail, links, and controlled emphasis |
| Dark panel | `#0b3032` | Diagnostic/review surface and dark text contrast |
| Type | IBM Plex Sans, IBM Plex Mono, Newsreader | Operational copy, labels, and editorial headline only |

These are style-reference tokens from the current site stylesheet, not a claim about a separate published brand library.

## Reusable Composition

1. Open with one bounded product-operation problem, not a promise.
2. Show a thin six-stage rail: source packet, evidence labels, PRD/ICP, acceptance criteria, task handoff, review gate.
3. Place a clear FACT, INTERPRETATION, HYPOTHESIS, or GAP chip beside any non-factual statement.
4. Use one evidence-receipt card, redaction bar, review stamp, or before/after artifact panel.
5. End with a single safe CTA and the review-status footer.

## Accessibility And Safety Gate

- Use strong contrast, stable grid margins, short text blocks, and labels that do not depend on color alone.
- Use no screenshots, raw source text, customer logos, personal/account data, private URLs, fake dashboards, or copied social media.
- Use no claim of live provider, runtime, deployment, writeback, buyer proof, revenue, ROI, or customer outcome unless separately verified and approved.
- Do not create the asset if its proof depends on private material. Produce a public-safe specification or mark it blocked instead.
