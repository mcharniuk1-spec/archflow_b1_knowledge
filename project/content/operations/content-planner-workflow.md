# Content Planner Workflow

Status: planning workflow
Date: 2026-07-10

## Goal

Turn reviewed ArchFlow work into a public-safe content backlog and calendar.

## Stages

1. Evidence intake.
   - Read run handout, review gate, and source inventory.
   - Mark each claim as FACT, INTERPRETATION, HYPOTHESIS, or GAP.

2. Topic scoring.
   - Score ICP fit, pain relevance, currentness, differentiation, source strength, CTA fit, and risk.
   - Reject topics that need private evidence or unsupported buyer proof.

3. Calendar mapping.
   - Assign items to 70 percent static, 20 percent carousel/checklist/template, and 10 percent short video or scripted demo.
   - Every short video needs a static companion.

4. CTA mapping.
   - Match each item to a Knowledge Diagnostic, source-packet review, Product Knowledge Reliability Sprint, discovery call, or review checklist.
   - Do not use CTAs that imply live automation, deployment, or buyer proof before validation.

5. Review packet.
   - Attach source inventory.
   - List unsupported claims.
   - Send to AF Review before drafting.

## Topic Scoring Rubric

| Field | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| ICP fit | Generic | Product-adjacent | B2B SaaS product leader pain |
| Pain relevance | Abstract | Related | Enterprise answer, onboarding, departure, evidence, retrieval, or handoff failure |
| Source strength | Unsupported | One public signal | Two public signals or repo proof |
| Differentiation | Feature comparison only | Partial wedge | Maintained source/authority/review-gate wedge |
| CTA fit | No next action | Soft learning | Diagnostic or source-packet review |
| Risk | Unsafe | Needs edits | Public-safe after review |

Use only topics with a total score of 8 or higher and no unsafe risk flag.

## Save Locations

- Calendar: `project/content/calendar/`
- One-off planning run: `project/runs/E4/<date-run-name>/`
- Review gate: beside the calendar or run artifact.
