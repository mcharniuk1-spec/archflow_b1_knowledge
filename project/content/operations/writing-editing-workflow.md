# Writing And Editing Workflow

Status: planning workflow
Date: 2026-07-10

## Goal

Create public-safe ArchFlow company-voice drafts from approved content planner packets.

## Draft Sequence

1. Load the planner packet and evidence checklist.
2. Choose the format: static post, carousel, checklist, template, short video script, or companion static post.
3. Write a concise company-voice draft for B2B SaaS product leaders.
4. Add a claim table with FACT, INTERPRETATION, HYPOTHESIS, and GAP labels.
5. Remove unsupported demand, ROI, customer, revenue, or production-runtime claims.
6. Prepare an AF Review packet.

## Voice Rules

- Use direct product-operations language.
- Focus on source lineage, PRD clarity, review gates, acceptance criteria, backlog handoff, and decision memory.
- Do not write personal build-in-public diary content unless owner-approved.
- Do not overstate automation: Codex is the current operator; Jarvis/provider/writeback/deploy are gated unless proven.

## Format Rules

LinkedIn static:

- One sharp opening.
- One concrete problem.
- One source-boundary or review-gate lesson.
- One CTA.

Carousel:

- 5 to 7 slides.
- One claim per slide.
- Proof or gap marker on every slide.

X and Threads-style excerpts:

- Derived only from reviewed LinkedIn/company draft.
- No new claims.
- Link back to the approved artifact or CTA after owner approval.

Short video script:

- 30 to 60 seconds.
- One visual artifact concept.
- Static companion required.
- No raw screen or private-source recording.

## Review Gate

Publication remains blocked until AF Review and owner approval are recorded.
