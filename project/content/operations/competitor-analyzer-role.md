# Competitor Analyzer Role

Status: planning contract
Date: 2026-07-10

## Job

Map current alternatives and objections for the ArchFlow PRD automation service using public sources only.

## Current Alternative Categories

| Category | Examples | What They Usually Own | ArchFlow Wedge |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product management suites | Productboard, Aha, Cycle | Roadmaps, ideas, feedback, planning workflows | Source packet to reviewed PRD/task/KB handoff service |
| Insight and customer-intelligence repositories | Dovetail, BuildBetter | Research, customer signals, searchable insights | Translation of signals into delivery-ready PRD and acceptance criteria |
| AI PRD drafting tools | ChatPRD | Fast PRD/story/spec drafting and PM coaching | Evidence labels, review gates, backlog/KB handoff, and source-boundary discipline |
| Workspace AI and enterprise search | Notion AI, Glean, Dust | Search, agents, workspace context, knowledge access | Accountable product artifact production from approved sources |
| Human alternatives | Status quo, junior PM/BA, fractional consultant | Manual synthesis, rewrite loops, expert judgment | Productized diagnostic and reviewed delivery packet |

## Required Output

- Source inventory.
- Category map.
- Objection script.
- Differentiation hypothesis.
- Evidence gaps.
- Claims downgraded to FACT, INTERPRETATION, HYPOTHESIS, or GAP.

## Review Checks

- Public-source URL or repo artifact exists for each competitor claim.
- No claim relies on private account access or scraped social data.
- No demand, market-share, customer, revenue, or ROI claim is made without separate E5/E7 evidence.
- Product positioning is described as current-source observation, not final market truth.
- Cycle and any other source that cannot be extracted cleanly remains a GAP until verified.

## Recommended Objection Frames

Roadmap suite objection:

- "You already have planning software. The gap ArchFlow targets is the source-lineage and delivery-handoff step before the roadmap item is ready to build."

Insight repository objection:

- "Searchable research is useful, but someone still has to turn scattered signals into acceptance criteria, backlog, and decision memory."

AI PRD tool objection:

- "Drafting faster does not solve whether the PRD is evidence-backed, reviewed, and safe to hand to engineering."

Workspace AI objection:

- "Finding context is not the same as owning the product artifact and review gate."

Human alternative objection:

- "Manual synthesis works, but ArchFlow packages the repeatable parts into a diagnostic and reviewable handoff."
