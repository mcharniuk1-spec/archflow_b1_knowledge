## Contract rule

Every task must preserve two separate states:

- workflow status: Not started, Planning, Active, Review, Done, or Blocked;
- evidence state: Planned, Configured, Executed, Reviewed, Proven, Superseded, or Gap.

Done never upgrades an unsupported market, runtime, safety, ROI, or deployment claim. External action requires a target-specific owner approval, action receipt, and readback. The accountable role prepares/integrates; the independent reviewer may approve, repair, block, or stop but may not approve its own high-risk output.
